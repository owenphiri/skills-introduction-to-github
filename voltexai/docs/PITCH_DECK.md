## 1. VoltexAI
Africa's AI trading terminal **and** managed-alpha platform. One product line takes a
beginner from their first lesson to a funded prop account — or lets them invest
hands-off in a professionally managed program.

## 2. The problem
African retail traders are underserved:
- Foreign tools price in USD and ignore mobile-money rails (MTN, Airtel, M-Pesa).
- Beginners are buried in complexity and blow accounts without structure or risk discipline.
- Trustworthy, localized education + execution + capital rarely sit in one place.

## 3. The solution
A Claude-powered **AI trading terminal** that:
- Teaches ICT/SMC and risk management (Academy mode).
- Generates structured signals and reads chart screenshots (vision).
- Streams live markets and an algorithmic signal scanner.
- Accepts **card (Stripe) and mobile money (Flutterwave)** — Africa-first.

## 4. Product
**Web + mobile (PWA + native Expo app):**
AI Terminal · Live Markets board · Algorithmic Signal scanner · Chart Vision ·
Prop-firm & Broker directories · Managed-AUM program with an investor dashboard.
